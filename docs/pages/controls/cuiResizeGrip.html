cuiResizeGrip

Restores bottom-right resizing for borderless forms by providing a draggable resize grip.

Important Properties

Property Description
TargetForm The borderless form that will be resized when the grip is dragged.
GripTexture Determines whether the resize grip texture is drawn.
GripSize Controls the size of each square in the grip texture.
SkipBottomRightSquare Omits the bottom-right square from the grip texture.
Tip
The grip automatically stays aligned with the bottom-right corner of the target form, even when the form is resized.
