cuiLabel

A label that supports both horizontal and vertical text alignment, along with escaped newline and tab characters in its content.

Important Properties

Property Description
Content The text displayed by the control. Escaped sequences such as \n, \r\n, and \t are automatically converted into newlines and tabs.
HorizontalAlignment Controls the horizontal alignment of the text.
VerticalAlignment Controls the vertical alignment of the text.
Tip
Unlike the standard WinForms Label, this control supports vertical text alignment in addition to horizontal alignment.
