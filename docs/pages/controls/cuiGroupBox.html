cuiGroupBox

A lightweight container with a rounded border and an optional title displayed in its top-left corner.

Important Properties

Property Description
Content Sets the title displayed at the top of the group box.
Text An alias for Content, provided for compatibility with standard WinForms controls.
Rounding Controls the corner radius of the border.
Tip
Child controls can be added directly to the group box, just like with a standard Panel.

Caution
The control automatically adjusts its internal Padding to leave space for the title. Setting Padding manually has no lasting effect.
